Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 2026

1. Data We Collect

When you use Spendly, we collect the following information:

  • Account information — your name and email address when you register
  • Expense data — amounts, categories, dates, and descriptions you enter
  • Usage data — pages visited and actions taken within the app, used to improve the service

We do not collect payment information, government IDs, or any sensitive financial credentials.

2. How We Use Your Data

We use the data we collect solely to provide and improve the Spendly service:

  • To authenticate you and display your expense history
  • To generate summaries and category breakdowns within your account
  • To send transactional emails (e.g. password resets) if you request them
  • To identify and fix bugs or performance issues

We will never use your data for advertising, profiling, or resale.

3. Data Storage

Your data is stored on secured servers. We apply reasonable technical and organisational measures to protect it against unauthorised access, loss, or disclosure.

You can request deletion of your account and all associated data at any time. Upon request, your data will be permanently removed within 30 days, except where retention is required by law.

4. Third-Party Services

Spendly does not share your personal data with third-party advertising or analytics platforms. We may use third-party infrastructure providers (e.g. hosting or email delivery) who process data on our behalf under strict confidentiality agreements.

Any such providers are prohibited from using your data for their own purposes.

5. Contact Us

If you have questions about this Privacy Policy or wish to exercise your data rights (access, correction, or deletion), please reach out via the contact information in your account settings.

We aim to respond to all privacy-related requests within 7 business days.
